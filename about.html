Project Overview & Mission

AegisScreen AI was built to address the bottlenecks in technical recruitment. By applying natural language processing and neural-driven matching metrics, our mission is to eliminate repetitive manual screening and help recruiting teams make faster, unbiased candidate decisions.

Addressing the Recruitment Bottle-neck

A side-by-side comparison of traditional workflows against our optimized AI model.

The Problem

Sifting, Layout Inconsistencies, & Subconscious Bias

Recruiters spend an average of 15-20 minutes parsing each candidate resume. Differences in formats (multiple columns, text blocks, graphic charts) confuse legacy keyword ATS filters, causing highly skilled candidates to be filtered out due to bad parser optimization.

The Solution

AegisScreen AI Automated Parsing & Classifier

A unified semantic ingestion engine parses files (.pdf, .docx) in less than 3 seconds. It ignores styling formatting, extracts core skills, evaluates timeline details, and maps qualifications against role profiles to predict job suitability with high accuracy.
